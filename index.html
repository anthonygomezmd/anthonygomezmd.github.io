I'm an MD with clinical experience across Mexico and U.S. healthcare settings, currently focused on the quality and safety of AI-generated medical content.

My work involves evaluating AI responses the way I would evaluate a junior clinician's reasoning — checking for missed diagnoses, unsafe recommendations, guideline misalignment, and logical gaps that a non-clinician would never catch.

I've worked in resource-limited environments where you can't order every test, in medico-legal documentation where precision is non-negotiable, and in emergency triage where speed and accuracy have to coexist. That breadth is what I bring to AI evaluation.

Currently preparing for the USMLE while actively building my practice at the intersection of medicine and AI.
